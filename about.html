About the Project

Our project combines Biorock technology and a Semi-Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (SAUV) to restore coral reefs. Biorock uses electric currents to accelerate coral growth, while SAUVs handle coral transport and monitoring with precision and minimal environmental disturbance.

This hybrid method promotes faster reef recovery, reduces the need for divers, and enables scalable, eco-friendly restoration powered by renewable energy.

Meet the Team

We are Heliopolis Fuzzy Logic from Heliopolis Academy. We’re participating in the FLL Challenge: Submerged, where we apply real-world science and robotics to solve challenges facing our oceans.

With diverse skills in STEM and a shared passion for the environment, our team is excited to bring innovation to coral reef conservation.
